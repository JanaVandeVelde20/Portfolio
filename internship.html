During my internship I made a a new mobile app called the Companion App. This app was created to meet the growing need of employers for a faster, more convenient way to handle daily tasks directly from the workfloor. Instead of having to return to their offices and use a desktop computer, they can now perform essential actions, such as managing time-registrations and absences right from their phones which saves time and increases the efficiency on-site.

The main features of the new app include: quickly clocking employees in or out if they forget, adjusting time registrations, marking absences, scheduling available staff, contacting employees, and easily viewing who is currently working, scheduled, or being late.
