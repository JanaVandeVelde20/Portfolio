ERASMUS

Studying in Sweden

In my last year at Thomas More, I had the chance to study in Sweden at University West in Trollhättan. While I was there, I not only improved my technical skills but also got to know more about Swedish culture. I learned to enjoy nature, go hiking, and take part in outdoor life. I also discovered and really enjoyed the Swedish tradition of fika, where people take a break to relax and have coffee together.

What I have learned

During my time abroad, I gained valuable technical experience. In the course System Development for Mobile Applications, I learned more about Progressive Web Apps and React Native, which strengthened my skills in mobile development.

In the Immersive Media course, I learned how to create virtual environments using Twinmotion and Unreal Engine. We also used a Meta Quest headset to test our projects in VR, which was an exciting and valuable experience
