Internship assignment

Companion app

During my internship at Strobbo in Lommel, I helped build a new app called the Companion App. This app makes it easy for employers to do their daily tasks right on the workfloor, without needing a computer. With the app, they can see who is currently working, mark someone as absent, clock employees in or out, and quickly call or text an employee.
